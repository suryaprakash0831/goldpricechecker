🔢 India Price Formula
1
International SpotFetch XAU/XAG/XPT in USD/oz from 3 sources. Take median. Reject outliers >0.5% from median.
2
Convert to INR/gram(USD/oz ÷ 31.1035) × live USD/INR = INR per gram (international purity)
3
Add Indian Taxes× 1.125 (12.5% import duty) × 1.03 (3% GST) = INR/gram pre-premium
4
MCX PremiumAdd ₹250–350/10g MCX dealer margin based on volatility index
5
City Tax DeltaEach city has a % delta from MCX base (Karnataka 0%, Tamil Nadu +0.5%, etc.)
6
Karat Purity Factor24K=1.0, 22K=0.9167, 18K=0.75, 14K=0.5833 of 999-fine price
