About Gold Price in India

Gold prices in India change daily depending on international gold markets, the US dollar rate, crude oil prices, and import duties. The most commonly traded gold purity in India includes 24K gold (99.9%) and 22K gold (91.6%) used for jewellery. Cities like Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, and Chennai have slight variations in gold rates due to transportation costs and local taxes.
